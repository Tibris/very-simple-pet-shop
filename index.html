Who are we?

Tibris Pet Shop is clearly a pet shop...That's it!
Just kidding! We are not just a regular pet shop. We are animal lovers. Tibris Pet Shop started as a small project with the main goal; to rescue as many animals as possible. Unfortunately, in a world where everyone has to provide for themselves in order to survive, we transformed this project into a business. We do not like the fact that we 'sell' animals. That's a very inappropriate term to use when it comes to living beings.
We believe this explains the low price we put for our small friends.
